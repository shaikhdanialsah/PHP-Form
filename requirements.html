 Please read this before you proceed with your application.



1. This application form can be downloaded and submitted to Office/Satellite Office/Urban Transformation Center (UTC), Inland Revenu Board of Malaysia (LHDN) or filled in by online at the official portal for RRC. Any submission by using fax and email is PROHIBITED.

2. Information needs to be filled in English language only and every related field needs to be written in CAPITAL LETTER.

3. For the 2023 RCC recipients, you will need to update your information if there is any changes.

4. For the 2023 RCC recipients who do not update their information for 3 years straight since 2021,2022 and 2023 are required to do a quick update.

5. Applicants can check the status for your application on the official website for RCC.

6. For any further details, you may visit the nearest LHDN branch or contact on 1 -800-88-2727 / Ministry of Finance on 03-88824565/ 4566.



1  

REQUIREMENTS FOR APPLICANTS



  1. Household Category
    • Malaysian citizen who lives in Malaysia only; and
    • Married; or
    • Widow or widower with children who fulfill the requirements; and
    • Man or woman who works in Malaysia and earns RM 5000 or less per month.


  2. Bachelor Senior Citizens
    • Malaysian citizen who lives in Malaysia only; and
    • Widow or widower without children who fulfill the requirements; and
    • 60 years and above; and
    • Man or woman who works in Malaysia and earns RM 5000 or less per month.


  3. Bachelor
    • Malaysian citizen who lives in Malaysia only; and
    • Widow or widower without children who fulfill the requirements; and
    • Age range as below:
      • 21 until 59 years old ; or
      • 19 until 59 years old for people with disability ; and
    • Man or woman who works in Malaysia and earns RM 5000 or less per month.


2  

REQUIREMENTS FOR PARTNER



  1. Malaysian citizen with (MyKad) or possess Malaysian identity card such as (MyPR / MyKAS); or
  2. Individual who is not a Malaysian citizen(besides MyPR / MyKAS)


3  

REQUIREMENTS FOR CHILDREN



  1. Biological child (including illegitimate child) or children that has been registered with ; and
  2. Malaysia citizen that holds MyKAD or MyKID only; and
  3. Unemployed or does not have any source of income; and
  4. 18 years old and below; or
  5. 19 years old and above and a full time student at any public or private institutes of higher education
